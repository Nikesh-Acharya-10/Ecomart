Research & Comparison

Research and comparison are planned as the study of the available websites and comparison of their features with those of EcoMart as the way to learn the best design practices and to make the user experience more appropriate with the help of a better usability and sustainability.

Why This Research Matters?

Our eco mart webpage is dedicated to the analysis and comparison of our site design and functionality with other popular online commerce and sustainability-oriented sites like Amazon, Daraz, Etsy, Apple, and Patagonia. The primary objective of this page is to research the design and application of various web elements such as navigation bars, product pages, blogs, team section, and contact forms in websites in the real world. In this study, we did a close observation and analysis of the different popular websites and chose certain elements to compare them. These websites were used as screenshots and design inspirations to learn about how these websites are arranged, to use and to experience them. According to this analysis, EcoMart has adopted better and environmentally friendly design options, which are less complex, informative, and user-friendly. The research page is significant as it supports the choice in design applied in the EcoMart site. It also indicates the site is not created in a vacuum but that a lot of research and comparison has been undertaken to create the current site. Through learning and refining of the attributes that are weak in other existing platforms, EcoMart offers enhanced usability, understandable navigation, informative and a sustainable digital experience to its users.
